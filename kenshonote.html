Prize Contest Notebook

"Prize Contest Notebook" is an app designed to efficiently enjoy prize contests. Prize contests bring a bit of excitement to our lives, but efficiently organizing and managing this information can be quite challenging. This app allows users to centrally manage their contest entry status as 'Not Entered', 'Awaiting Results', 'Won/Lost', and prioritizes displaying contests whose deadlines are approaching. We hope this app will provide a bit of joy in the midst of a busy life.
